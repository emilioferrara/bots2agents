Three corrections worth knowing

The election-interference taxonomy is often cited from its 2024 SSRN preprint. The version of record is First Monday 30(6), 2025 — cite that one.

The ICWSM 2020 inverse-reinforcement-learning paper is frequently cited with a truncated title. The full title carries the subtitle “A Case Study of Russian Trolls in the 2016 US Election.”

The 2024 cross-platform operations paper exists as an SSRN preprint with a different author order. The version of record is First Monday 29(11): Minici, Cinus, Luceri, Ferrara.
